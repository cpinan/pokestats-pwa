PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 2026
PokéStats Calculator does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

1. NO PERSONAL DATA

PokéStats Calculator is a free, offline-capable Progressive Web App (PWA) for calculating Pokémon stats. We do not collect any personally identifiable information. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no user profiles.

2. LOCAL STORAGE

The app stores two small items in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device:

  • pokestats-lang — your language preference (e.g. "en" or "es")
  • pokestats-saves — your saved Pokémon builds (up to 20 entries)

You can clear this data at any time via your browser settings → Clear site data.

3. INDEXEDDB (OFFLINE CACHE)

When you look up a Pokémon, its data is cached in your browser's IndexedDB under the database name pokestats-db. This allows the app to work offline after the first lookup. The cache contains only public Pokémon game data fetched from PokéAPI — no personal information.

4. SERVICE WORKER

The app uses a Service Worker to cache its own files (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, images) for offline use. This cache contains only the app's source files — no user data.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The app may load resources from the following third parties:

  • Google Fonts — loads the Press Start 2P and Rajdhani typefaces. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • PokéAPI (pokeapi.co) — public read-only Pokémon data API. No personal data is sent.

If Google Analytics or a crash-reporting service is enabled in a future version, this policy will be updated to reflect that. No such tracking is active by default.

6. COOKIES

PokéStats Calculator does not use cookies of any kind.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

This app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, it is safe for all ages.

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. CONTACT
