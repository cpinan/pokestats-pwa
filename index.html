STAT FORMULAS
Gen III–IX uses two formulas depending on the stat type.
HP = floor((2×Base + IV + floor(EV/4)) × Lv / 100) + Lv + 10
Stat = floor((floor((2×Base + IV + floor(EV/4)) × Lv / 100) + 5) × Nature)
HP = floor((Base + IV) × 2 + floor(√EV)) × Lv / 100) + Lv + 10
Stat = floor((Base + IV) × 2 + floor(√EV)) × Lv / 100) + 5
In Gen I/II, IVs are called DVs and max at 15. EVs are Stat Exp and max at 65535. The formula uses √(StatExp).
Boosted stat: ×1.1 (rounded down)
Neutral stat: ×1.0
Reduced stat: ×0.9 (rounded down)
HP max: floor((2×255 + 31 + 63) × 1) + 110 = 714
Other max: floor((2×255 + 31 + 63 + 5) × 1.1) = 1206 (SpAtk, Nidoking)
